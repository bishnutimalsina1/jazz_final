    UNT Jazz 75th

    Anniversary

    The College of Music and the University of North Texas celebrate the 75th anniversary of North Texas Jazz. As one of the leading programs in jazz education, we invite you to celebrate with many events and performances.

    The first laboratory band was formed in 1946 to perform student arrangements, part of a curriculum proposed in the master's thesis of Gene Hall, who would direct the program. Later named jazz studies, it was the first degree of its kind in the world and the program soon made a name for itself around globally, particularly with its One O'Clock Lab Band®, which has earned seven Grammy nominations and toured internationally.
