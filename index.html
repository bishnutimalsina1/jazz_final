UNT Jazz 75th Anniversary Schedule of Events

    Friday–Sunday, October 1, 2, and 3, 2021

    Denton Arts & Jazz Festival, UNT Jazz 75th Anniversary Kick off, Celebration of Leon Breeden’s centennial birthday on the UNT Jazz Showcase stage sponsored by the Law Offices of Tim Powers throughout the weekend.

    Tuesday, November 23, 2021

    One O’Clock Lab Band 61st Annual Fall Concert featuring UNT alumni guest artist

    Wednesday, December 15, 2021

    Two O’Clock Lab Band performance at Midwest Clinic, Chicago, Illinois

    Wednesday–Saturday, January 5–8, 2022

    Jazz Education Network (JEN) Conference, Hyatt Regency, Dallas with multiple performances, workshops and clinics by UNT ensembles and jazz faculty. Also planned is an alumni reception. The UNT College of Music is Presenting Sponsor for this conference.

    Various dates (Tuesdays, February thru April)

    UNT Jazz Lecture Series featuring UNT jazz alumni

    Tuesday–Friday, March 8–11, 2022

    28th Annual Glenn E. Gomez International Artists Endowment for Jazz Studies Residency and Concert with Kurt Elling, featuring world premiere of composition by Rich DeRosa.

    Saturday, April 30, 2022

    Lab Band Madness Concert, UNT Jazz 75th Anniversary celebration

    Wednesday–Sunday, October 19–23, 2022

    UNT Jazz 75th Anniversary Reunion
